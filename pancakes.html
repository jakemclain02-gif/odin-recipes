Pancakes

Pancakes are a delicious breakfast made with flour, eggs, milk, and butter.

Pancakes are a great breakfast, fluffy and golden-brown on the outside, with a soft, delicious inside. They are often served with syrup, butter, and fresh fruit.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. In another bowl, beat the eggs and milk together.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients until just mixed.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown on the other side.
