Lasagna

Lasagna is a delicious Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

Lasagna is a comforting Italian baked casserole made of wide, flat pasta sheets layered with rich meat or tomato sauce, and melted cheeses like mozzarella, ricotta, and Parmesan. It is a hearty, layered masterpiece of savory flavors.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package directions.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef until browned.
  4. Add the tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Layer the pasta, meat sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
