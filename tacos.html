Tacos

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish made with a folded or rolled tortilla filled with various ingredients.

Tacos are a delicious dish that can be filled with a variety of ingredients, including meats, beans, cheese, and vegetables. They are often topped with salsa, guacamole, and sour cream.

Ingredients:

  • Tortillas
  • Ground beef or chicken
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Salsa

Instructions:

  1. Cook the ground beef or chicken in a skillet until fully cooked.
  2. Warm the tortillas in a separate pan or microwave.
  3. Assemble the tacos by placing the cooked meat on the tortillas and adding desired toppings such as cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, and salsa.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!
